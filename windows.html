It's fairly easy to get JRSST to work with Windows with the options you want. Download the JRSST.exe and double click on it. It should run in "demo mode" and display a BBC world news site. To specify your own arguments, create a shortcut to the JRSST.exe file -- right click on the downloaded JRSST.exe and choose "Create Shortcut". Right click on the shortcut and add the options and RSS feeds you want to the end of the 'Target:', after the double quotes:

Click "OK". Now double clicking on the shortcut should bring JRSST up with the options you specified.
